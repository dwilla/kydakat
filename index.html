About Kyda Kat

Kat is an artist born and raised in New England. With classical training in drawing, inks, and charcoal she specializes in oils and acrylics. With a focus on murals and spacial design her art aims to invoke a sense of wonder at the simple, yet awe-inspiring brilliance inherently hidden in even the most mundane spaces.
Kyda is Kat's freedom of creation. She believes that everyone can and should create art. Every being on this planet is capable, and within them lives an entire universe ready to explore. Kyda Kat Art LLC is here to help facilitate the transformation of those universal ideas into tangible creations.

Inspiration

She believes that art has the power to transcend boundaries and bring people together. Through her murals, she aims to create a sense of unity and connection within communities and the spaces they embody. Whether it's vibrant surrealism or a serene natural landscape, her artwork has the ability to captivate viewers and transport them to a different world.
As a passionate advocate for outdoor conservation, she often finds inspiration for her creations amongst the wilderness and natural landscape. The colors in the sun's rays, the textures of a tree's twisting bark, or the movements flowing within a mountain stream find their way into her artistic creations. She believes that nature holds endless inspiration and seeks to inspire others to appreciate it's every element.

Community

Collaboration is an integral part off Kat's creative process. She enjoys working closely with clients to understand their visions and passions for an idea to better bring them into creation. From concept, to review, to creation, she aims to help evoke a sense of wonder and deeper appreciation for the creative process. Whether it's a mural in a community space, or a personal commissioned work, or original inspiration.
